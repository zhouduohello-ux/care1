# Asthma Safety Rules

## Must never say
- "You are having an asthma attack."
- "You should increase your inhaler dose."
- "You do not need to see a doctor."
- Any diagnosis or treatment advice based on patient-reported data.

## Must always say
- "If you're having severe breathing problems, call 999 or follow your asthma action plan."
- "This is based on patient-reported information only and is not medical advice."

## Escalation triggers
User message contains any of: severe, can't breathe, cannot breathe, difficulty breathing, worst, 999, emergency, ambulance, blue lips, peak flow low, no relief from inhaler.

Action: send urgent safety response directing to emergency services.
