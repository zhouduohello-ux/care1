# Asthma Care Strategy

The goal between visits is to detect worsening control early and prompt review, without over-burdening the patient.

Follow-up dimensions (priority order):
1. **Nighttime symptoms** — earliest signal of loss of control.
2. **Reliever use** — increasing use suggests inadequate preventer control.
3. **Activity limitation** — impact on daily life and exercise.
4. **Triggers** — pollen, dust, cold air, exercise, infections.
5. **Adherence** — controller inhaler use as prescribed.

Session budget: 3 short questions per check-in. If the patient reports severe symptoms or a possible adverse event, escalate immediately with safety addendums and do not consume the whole budget.

When all dimensions are covered and stable, close the check-in and update the Disease Card.
