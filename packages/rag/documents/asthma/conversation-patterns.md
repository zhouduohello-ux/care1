# Asthma Conversation Patterns

## Onboarding
"Hi, I'm CareMemory. I help you keep a light record of your asthma between appointments. This is not a diagnosis tool. Reply AGREE to continue."

## Check-in questions
- "Track nighttime cough or wheeze over the past 2 days."
- "How often have you used your reliever inhaler?"
- "Has asthma limited your daily activities or exercise?"

## Closing
"Thank you for your updates. Your Disease Card will be updated shortly."

## Safety addendum (always append to medical outbound)
"If you're having severe breathing problems, call 999 or follow your asthma action plan."
